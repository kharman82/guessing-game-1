The Life of Joe

This is an autobiography of yours truly, Joe Pennock.

About me

Hello! My name is Joe Pennock. I am 32 years old and I was born and raised in the Pacific Northwest, the most beautiful place on earth! I've been married for 5 years now to my lovely wife Meghan. We don't have kids yet, though that will probably start to change here in the near future. I have a dog named Sawyer who is the best boy. He can be a little mischievous sometimes, but I still manage to love him anyway. I'm currently enrolled at Code Fellows on the path to becoming a master code wizard.

Education and Work Experience

Before coming to Code Fellows, I hadn't been in school in 13 years. I graduated from Kamiak High School in Mukilteo, Washington in 2005. After taking a year off, I completed one year at Seattle Bible College in Seattle, Washington. And now here I am again in school over a decade later!

My most recent job was working as a Class A professional driver. I drove trucks for a little over 3 years. Prior to that I was an aerospace machinist for 5 years. I was a union laborer in Seattle, WA prior to that, which I did from the point I left Seattle Bible College until I started working in Aerospace.

My Goals for the Future

My immediate goal for the future is to finish the Code 201 course at Code Fellows. My plan is to go on to complete their Code 301 and then eventually their Code 401: Advanced Software Development in Python course. once I finish school, I would love to work at a local startup and get some real world experience in the tech industry. Once I have a little work experience under my belt, I want to pursue jobs more in the AI/ML field. I love the possibilities presented by the advances in AI. I also have an interest in augmented and virtual reality technologies. I would love to play even a small role in the development of a working full-dive virtual reality environment.
